The Practice

Twenty years of independent
counsel.

Founded in 2004 on a single premise — that most Australians receive mortgage advice shaped by the broker's commercial interests, not their own.

"A broker's first obligation is not to a lender. It is to the client — and to the years of financial consequence that follow."

J.P. Bergman, Founding Principal
Independence
No bank ownership. No volume bonuses.

Access to more than forty lenders — spanning major banks, mutual institutions, and non-bank specialists — ensures every recommendation is made on objective merit alone. No preferred lender arrangements. No commercial incentives to place loans with particular institutions.

Selectivity
Deliberately small. By design.

The practice has deliberately remained small. Selectivity in clientele permits a depth of service that volume-driven operations cannot replicate. When you engage J.P. Bergman, you deal with the principal directly — not a team, not a processing centre.

Accountability
One adviser. One point of contact. Always.

The same person who assesses your file submits it, negotiates on your behalf, coordinates settlement, and reviews your loan annually thereafter. Rate complacency is the most common cost in mortgage management — this practice eliminates it.

Lender Panel
The full market, not a curated shelf.

Commonwealth Bank, Westpac, ANZ, NAB, Macquarie, ING, Suncorp, Bank of Melbourne — plus 32 specialist and non-bank lenders. Options are never constrained by what generates the highest trail commission.

Process

From first conversation
to settlement — and beyond.

A structured process built around your timeline, your objectives, and your right to understand every decision made on your behalf.

I

Discovery & Assessment

Your position and objectives understood in full. No credit enquiries at this stage. Strategy is set before any lender is approached.

II

Lender Selection

Your file assessed against suitable institutions. Rate is one variable — serviceability treatment, policy flexibility, and approval timeframes carry equal weight.

III

Application & Negotiation

Documentation collated, application submitted, rate negotiated on your behalf. Direct lender relationships allow access to pricing unavailable through standard lodgement.

IV

Settlement & Annual Review

Full coordination through to settlement. Your loan reviewed annually against the market — rate complacency costs most borrowers, and this practice prevents it.
